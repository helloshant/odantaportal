Our Journey of Connection

From a small startup with big dreams to India's most trusted broadband provider, our story is one of innovation, perseverance, and unwavering commitment to our customers.

2018

The Beginning

Founded by a team of telecom veterans with a vision to make high-speed internet accessible to every Indian household. Started operations in Murshidabad with 100 customers.

2020

Rapid Growth

Expanded to 20+ districts across West Bengal, serving 4,000+ customers. Launched our customer portal and introduced transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

2022

Technology Leadership

Deployed fiber-optic infrastructure across 20+ districts. Introduced AI-powered network optimization and launched our award-winning mobile app.

2025

Market Leadership

Today, we serve 4,000+ customers across 20+ districts with 99.9% uptime. Recognized as India's most customer-centric ISP with multiple industry awards.
